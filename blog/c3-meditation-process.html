Phase 0 Week 3 - The Meditation Process.


What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2 of Search Inside Yourself?

I already have had a fair bit of experience with meditation, so what was mentioned in chapter 2 and the video was familiar to me.

What new things have you learnt about mediation?

The idea of Meta attention, which is the ability to realize you have been distracted in the first place. It differs from holding attention. The way Meng explained this was really insightful.

Also the notions of self and what is self, was really fascinating.

Did any of the suggested mediation techniques stand out to you?

I had heard about the lotus pose before, and meng reaffirmed its importance by explaining why it is so well regarded – i.e., its useful as its one of the best poses for remaining both relaxed and alert at the same time. Unfortunately, it’s quite a difficult pose to get into as a westerner, as we are used to sitting in chairs, not on the floor. It’s something I want to utilize in the future though.

Other musings on Meditation, and how did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

I am up to 15 days in a row of solid meditation. All this reading of Search Inside Yourself has really motivated me!

In regard to my Website:
To be completely honest, I went out the window a little bit with regard to the amount of time spent on rebuilding. I spent almost 11 hours on it, nearing double the amount of time I was meant to take. A lot of this was actually wrapping my head around skeleton, and troubleshooting though. But I was starting to get a little perfectionistic towards the end, and there are still things that bug me about it. I need to try to stop comparing my site to others websites!
